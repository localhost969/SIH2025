SIH 2025 Problem Statement ID: 25080

Document Intelligence for KMRL

Paper Sense is a consolidated platform to summarize, route, and govern incoming documents across departments — enabling timely action and audit-ready traceability.

Explore Solution See the Problem

Government of Kerala

Platform Snapshot

  • • Smart priority inbox with role-based routing
  • • Instant summaries with links to original documents
  • • Cross-department alerts and audit trails
  • • Bilingual document support (English/Malayalam)

The Problem

  • • Thousands of daily documents from email, Maximo, SharePoint, WhatsApp, and scans.
  • • Delays in extracting actionable information impacting operations.
  • • Departmental silos cause duplicated effort and missed updates.
  • • Compliance risk from buried regulatory updates (CMRS & MoHUA).
  • • Knowledge loss during transfers/retirements; bilingual complexity.

Our Solution

  • • Instant, grounded summaries with links back to originals.
  • • Role-based routing to a smart inbox for Controllers, Engineering, Finance, HR.
  • • Single source of truth with full traceability and audit logs.
  • • Cross-department alerts for relevant teams and deadlines.
  • • Bilingual support with embedded tables and images.

Unique Advantages

  • Local-first deployment (data stays inside)
  • Context-aware for metro operations
  • Ready integrations: Maximo, email, SharePoint

Planned Stack

  • Python · FastAPI
  • Mistral OCR · Mistral LLM
  • PostgreSQL/MySQL · React (dashboards)
